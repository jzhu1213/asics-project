TeamViewer is a remote access and support tool that enables employees to connect to their computers and devices from virtually anywhere. At ASICS, the IT Team utilizes TeamViewer to assist employees who are working remotely. When an employee encounters a technical issue and needs help troubleshooting, they can request support from IT. Using TeamViewer, the IT Team can securely connect to the employee's computer and resolve the issue directly.

ASICS Employees

ASICS Employees who request remote assistance from the IT Team will need to provide their TeamViewer ID and Password, pictured right. This will allow an IT member to control the remote computer and troubleshoot the issue.

IT Team

IT Team members can input an ASICS employee's TeamViewer ID and password, giving them remote control of the employee's computer, allowing them to troubleshoot the issue and resolve it remotely.
