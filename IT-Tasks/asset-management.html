Asset Tracking Overview

This section provides guidance on managing IT assets including hardware inventory, warranty tracking, and equipment assignments. Use the tools and processes below to maintain accurate asset records.

Step-by-Step Guides

Step 1: Access the asset management system.

Log into the asset tracking portal using your credentials. Navigate to the main dashboard to view current inventory status.

Step 2: Update asset information.

Enter new equipment details, update warranty information, or modify assignment records as needed. Ensure all fields are completed accurately.

Step 3: Generate reports and track lifecycle.

Create inventory reports, check warranty expiration dates, and monitor equipment assignments. Use filters to view specific asset categories.
