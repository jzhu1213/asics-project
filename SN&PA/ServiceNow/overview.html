What is ServiceNow?

ServiceNow is our comprehensive IT service management platform that streamlines how we handle IT requests, track assets, and manage workflows. It serves as a centralized hub for all IT-related activities, making it easier to submit requests, track their progress, and access self-service resources.

Main Dashboard

The ServiceNow dashboard is your home base for all IT activities. Here you can see your open tasks, recent requests, and important announcements. The dashboard is customizable, allowing you to add widgets that fit your specific workflow and needs.

Dashboard Features:

  • My Requests: View all your submitted tickets and their current status
  • Recent Activity: See updates on your requests and any new assignments
  • Quick Actions: Common tasks like submitting new requests or checking asset status
  • Announcements: Important IT updates and system maintenance notifications

Asset Management

ServiceNow provides comprehensive asset management capabilities. You can view and manage your IT assets, such as laptops, monitors, and accessories. The platform allows you to check asset status, request new equipment, or report issues directly from the interface.

Asset Features:

  • Asset Inventory: View all devices assigned to you or your department
  • Warranty Tracking: Check warranty status and expiration dates
  • Maintenance History: See past repairs and maintenance records
  • Request New Assets: Submit requests for new equipment or replacements

Knowledge Base

ServiceNow includes a comprehensive knowledge base with self-help articles, troubleshooting guides, and best practices. This resource can help you resolve common issues without needing to submit a ticket.

Knowledge Base Benefits:

  • Self-Service Solutions: Find answers to common IT questions
  • Step-by-Step Guides: Detailed instructions for various processes
  • Best Practices: Learn optimal ways to use company systems
  • Frequently Asked Questions: Quick answers to common issues

Request Management

The request management system allows you to submit various types of IT requests, from simple password resets to complex hardware installations. All requests are tracked and prioritized based on impact and urgency.

Request Types:

  • Incident Reports: Report problems with existing systems or equipment
  • Service Requests: Request new services, access, or equipment
  • Change Requests: Request modifications to existing systems
  • Access Requests: Request access to new applications or systems

Navigation Tips

ServiceNow uses an intuitive navigation system with a main menu on the left side of the screen. Here are some tips for efficient navigation:

Navigation Features:

  • Favorites: Pin frequently used items to your favorites for quick access
  • Search: Use the global search to quickly find tickets, assets, or knowledge articles
  • Filters: Use filters to narrow down lists and find specific items
  • Breadcrumbs: Use breadcrumb navigation to understand your current location

Best Practices

To get the most out of ServiceNow, follow these best practices:

General Tips:

  • Provide Detailed Information: When submitting requests, include as much relevant detail as possible
  • Use Appropriate Categories: Select the correct category for your request to ensure proper routing
  • Check Knowledge Base First: Search for existing solutions before submitting new requests
  • Update Contact Information: Keep your contact details current for timely communication
  • Follow Up Appropriately: Use the ticket system for updates rather than creating new requests
