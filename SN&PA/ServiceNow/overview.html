Dashboard

The ServiceNow dashboard is your home base. Here you can see your open tasks, recent requests, and important announcements. You can customize widgets to fit your workflow.

Assets

View and manage your IT assets, such as laptops, monitors, and accessories. You can check asset status, request new equipment, or report issues directly from the platform.
