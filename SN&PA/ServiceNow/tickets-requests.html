Submitting a Ticket or Request

  1. Go to the ServiceNow portal and log in.
  2. Click "Create New Ticket" or "Submit Request".
  3. Fill in the required details and provide as much information as possible.
  4. Submit your ticket/request and note the reference number.

Tracking & Updating

Check the status of your tickets and requests from your dashboard. You can add comments, upload files, or close tickets when resolved.

FAQ

  • How do I escalate a ticket? Contact IT support or use the "Escalate" option in your ticket view.
  • What if I submitted the wrong request? Add a comment to your ticket or contact IT to update or close it.
  • How do I provide more info? Open your ticket and use the comment or attachment feature.
