Submit any Tickets & Requests to the provided link. This page will walk you through the process of submitting a ticket or request, as well as how to track the status of your request.

Step-by-Step: Submitting a Ticket or Request

Step 1

Go to the Tickets & Requests link above. You can also find this page by going to the ANA Connect Home> page, clicking the IT Service Now quick link, then the Get Help with IT link.

Step 2

You will be prompted to fill out the following fields:

  • Opened for: Should automatically be your name
  • Impact: How many people are affected by this issue?
  • Urgency: How urgent is this issue?
  • What is affected: What device does this issue affect?
  • Short description: A short description of the issue
  • Long description: A longer description of the issue if needed
  • On which phone number or extension can we reach you?: Contact information

After filling out the fields, Submit the ticket.

Step 3

Once the ticket is subitted, the IT Team will receive and review it. An email chain will be created for the ticket, and you will be notified with any updates.

Description

The ServiceNow Tickets & Requests system is your primary tool for reporting IT issues, requesting new equipment or software, and seeking general IT support. Submitting a ticket ensures your request is tracked, prioritized, and handled by the appropriate IT staff. You can monitor the status of your requests and receive updates directly via email or within the ServiceNow portal.

Use this system for:

  • Reporting hardware or software problems
  • Requesting new devices, software, or access
  • General IT inquiries or support needs
  • Tracking the progress and resolution of your requests

FAQ

  • How do I check the status of my ticket?
    Log in to ServiceNow and navigate to "My Tickets" or check your email for status updates from the IT team.
  • What information should I include in my ticket?
    Provide as much detail as possible, including device type, error messages, steps to reproduce the issue, and your contact information.
  • Can I update or add information to an existing ticket?
    Yes, you can add comments or attachments to your ticket in ServiceNow, or reply to the ticket email chain.
  • What if my issue is urgent?
    Mark the ticket as "High Urgency" and, if necessary, follow up with an email to the IT team. If you're in office, stop by the IT room and ask for assistance.
