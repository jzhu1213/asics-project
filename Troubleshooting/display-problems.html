Display appears stretched, blurry, or doesn't fit screen properly.

Step-by-Step Solution:

  2. Select correct monitor
  3. Choose recommended resolution
  4. Update graphics drivers if needed

üì∑Camera Issues

Webcam not working in Teams, Zoom, or other applications.

Step-by-Step Solution:

  1. Solution 1:
    • Open settings and search for "camera"
    • Make sure the app has access to your camera
    • Make sure the cameria isn't disabled or removed
  2. Solution 2:
    • Open Device Manager app and find your camera
    • Disable then re-enable the camera (may need admin credentials)
  3. Solution 3:
    • Open settings and navigate to "Check for updates"
    • Hit the check for updates button and download/install any available updates
    • Restart your computer once updates are completed

‚ö°Screen Flickering/Outage

Display flickers, flashes, or won't turn on.

Step-by-Step Solution:

  1. Hard reset your computer (Hold power button for at least 10 seconds, then press to turn on)
  2. Check battery power and cable connections
  3. Check for Windows updates
