Common Solutions:

Outlook Won't Open or Can't Login

Common Solutions:

  • Force Close: End Outlook process in Task Manager
  • Reset Password: Reset password through Outlook's reset portal
  • Reinstall: Uninstall and reinstall the Outlook application

  • Keep Outlook updated
  • Regularly restart your computer
  • Don't force close Outlook when syncing

üîß General Communication Issues

Account, Mobile & Security

Password & Account Issues:

  • Use website password reset portal
  • Contact IT helpdesk for immediate assistance
  • Verify your identity with security questions
  • Check if account is active and not expired
  • Verify correct username format

Mobile Device Setup:

Email Setup on Mobile:

  1. Download Outlook app from app store
  2. Enter your work email address
  3. Use company credentials to sign in
  4. Enable notifications as needed

Teams Mobile Setup:

  1. Download Teams app
  2. Sign in with work account
  3. Grant necessary permissions
  4. Test audio/video functionality
  5. Configure notification preferences

Spam & Security:

Spam Management:

  • Flag and Report emails as phishing
  • Add trusted senders to safe list
  • Beware of Mimecasts phising tests
  • Check spam folder regularly
  • Update email rules and filters

Security Best Practices:

  • Never share passwords
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails
  • Log out when using shared computers
