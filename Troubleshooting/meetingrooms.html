Guide for booking a meeting room.

Booking a Meeting Room through Teams/Outlook

Step 1: In the Teams or Outlook Calendar, create a New Event how you usually would.

Open your Teams or Outlook app, navigate to Calendar, and click the "New Event" button. A screen similar to the one pictured should pop up.

Step 2: In Add a room or location, select Browse all rooms, choose the Boston building, then your preferred room.

Click on the "Add a room or location" line, "Browse all rooms", find "Boston" in the buildings list, then choose your room.
