Boston WiFi Connectivity Overview

ASICS Employee Devices

  • Step 1: Click on the wireless icon in the taskbar.
  • Step 2: Connect to AAG Boston.
  • Step 3: Enter password: WantItMore@ASICS.

ASICS Guests

  • Step 1: Click on the wireless icon in the taskbar.
  • Step 2: Connect to AAG - Guests.
  • Step 3: Enter password: More@ASICS!.

ASICS Employee Personal Devices

  • Step 1: Click on the wireless icon in the taskbar.
  • Step 2: Connect to Onitsuka.
  • Step 3: Enter password: Stop@Never!.
